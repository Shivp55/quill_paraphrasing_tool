Question

Calculate the electric field at the center of a square 52.5 cm on a side if one corner is occuppied by a +45.0 uC charge and the other three are occupied by -27.0 uC charge


Answer

E1=k/d2(q1+q2)

    =9*109/(.5252/2)*(45+27)*10-6

    =4.702*106 N/C

E2=k/d2(q3-q4)

    =0

    ∴E=√(E12+E22) =E1=4.702*106 N/C directed from the +ve to -ve charge along the diagonal
