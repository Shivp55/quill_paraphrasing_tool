Question

The total electric flux from a cubical box 28.0 cm on a side is 1.45 x 10^3 N x m^2/ C. What charge is enclosed by the box


Answer

Total answers posted by the expert is: 1696

Assuming the total flux coming through
the entire box: All sides:
From Gauss's law:
Flux = ? E .dA = q/eo = 1.84*10^3 N*m^2/C
q = Flux * eo = 1.84*10^3*8.85*10^-12 =1.6284*10^-8 C
If "1.84*10^3 N*m^2/C" is coming through each side then
qt = q*6
