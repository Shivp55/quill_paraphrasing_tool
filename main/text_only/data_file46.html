Question

You are to drive to an interview in another town, at a distance of 340 km on an expressway. The interview is at 11:15 a.m. You plan to drive at 100 km/h, so you leave at 8:00 a.m. to allow some extra time. You drive at that speed for the first 100 km, but then construction work forces you to slow to 42.0 km/h for 41.0 km. What would be the least speed (in km/h) needed for the rest of the trip to arrive in time for the interview?
I had guessed 100.708 km but it was incorrect.
Any help would be appreciated.
:)


Answer

Total answers posted by the expert is: 6001

total time you have = 3 h 15 m  = 195 min  = 3.25 hour 

time taken in 1st 100km = d/v = 100/100 = 1 h 

then 41 km by 42km/h   t = 41/42 = 0.976 h    = 0.976 x 60 = 58.57 min 

rest distance = 340 - 100 -41 = 199 km 

time you have = 3.25 - 1 - 0.976 = 1.274 hour

speed = distance / time = 199/1.274 = 156.2 km/h
