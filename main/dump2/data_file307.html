Question

X has a normal distribution with a mean of 80.0 and a standard deviation of 3.5. Find the following probabilities:

(A) P(x < 75.0)
(B) P(75.0 < x < 82.0)
(C) P(x > 88.0)

Here is what I got for A but I am lost from here. Please help
P(x < 75.0)
=P((X-mean)/s <(75-80)/3.5)
=P(Z< -1.43)
= 0.0764
