Question

a single playing card is picked at random from a well-shuffled ordinary deck of 52 cards.
A=king picked
B=ace picked
C=heart picked
check wheteher,
1)A and B
2)B and C
3)A and C
are dependent or independent?


Answer

Total answers posted by the expert is: 790

p(A) = 4/52 = 1/13
p(B) = 4/52 = 1/13
p(C) = 13/52 = 1/4

1) p(A and B) = 0 != p(A) * p(B) --- hence dependent
2) p(B and C) = p(ace of heart) = 1/52 = 1/13 * 1/4 = p(B) * p(C)
hence independent.
3) p(A and C) = p(king of heart) = 1/52 = 1/13 * 1/4 = p(A) * p(C)
hence independent
