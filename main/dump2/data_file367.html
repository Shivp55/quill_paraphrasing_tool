Question

The size of a beaver population at a national park increases at the rate of 5.1% per year. If the size of the current population is 151, find how many beavers there should be in 4 years. Use the function f(x) = 151e^0.051t and round to the nearest whole number
A) 187 B) 185 C)189 D)183


Answer

Total answers posted by the expert is: 2989

The correct answer is 151 (1 + 0.051)^4 which is about 184.24 or 184 to the nearest whole number.

To answer questions like these use P (1 + A/100)^Y
where P is the starting value
A is the percentage increase per year
and Y is the number of years.
