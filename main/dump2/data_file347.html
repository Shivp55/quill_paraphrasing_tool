Question

Which of the experimental results for the photoelectric effects suggest that light can display particle-like behavior?


Answer

Total answers posted by the expert is: 5395

It is determined from work function and ejection of electron from metal surface.

Just hitting the metal surface with any light or photon does not emit the surface electron from metal.

The fact that a definite frequency of of light or photon higher than the work function of metal can eject an electronic proves that the energy of photons are given in discrete packets of fixed energy which is particle like nature.
