Question

A manufacturer estimates that if each shipment of raw materials contains x units, the total cost in dollars of obtaining and storing the years supply of raw materials will be C(x)=2x+(80000/x).

a. Find all vertical and horizontal asymtotes of the graph C(x).

b. As x gets larger the term 80000/x in C(x) gets smaller. What does this say about the relationship between the cost curve y=C(x) and the line y=2x?

or

C(x)=(2x^2+80000) / x

make denominator = 0

x = 0


so, at x = 0, there are vertical asymptotes
